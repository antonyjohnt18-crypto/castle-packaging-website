About Castle Packaging

Manufacturer of paper bags, covers, cups and tissues, based in Bengaluru.

Who We Are

Paper packaging, made to order

Castle Packaging manufactures paper bags, paper covers, paper cups and paper tissues for businesses that need reliable supply and consistent quality — with custom printing available across the entire range.

What we manufacture

Paper bags in kraft, recycled and virgin material; paper covers; paper cups; and paper tissues across semi soft, soft and virgin cellulose fibre grades. Every product can be custom printed with your branding.

Who we serve

Retailers, food and beverage businesses, distributors and brands across Bengaluru and beyond who need paper packaging in bulk, with or without custom printing.

Our Mission & Vision

Why we do what we do

Our Mission

To manufacture reliable, high-quality paper packaging — bags, covers, cups and tissues — that gives businesses a genuinely sustainable, brand-ready alternative to plastic, backed by consistent supply and on-time delivery.

Our Vision

To be Bengaluru's most trusted name in paper packaging — known for quality, dependability, and a genuine commitment to sustainable manufacturing as demand for eco-friendly packaging keeps growing.

Our Values

What guides how we work

Paper-first, sustainability-minded

We offer recycled and kraft options alongside virgin material, so you can choose what fits your product and your sustainability goals.

Branding built in

Custom printing is available on every product we make, so your packaging can carry your logo and branding, not just your product.

Direct from the manufacturer

As manufacturers, we work directly with you on specification, quantity and timelines, rather than routing your order through a reseller.
