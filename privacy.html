Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your information.

Last updated: 21 July 2026

Introduction

Castle Packaging ("we", "us", "our") operates the website www.castlepkg.com. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect when you visit our site or contact us, how we use it, and the choices you have. By using this website, you agree to the practices described here.

Information We Collect

We collect information in the following ways:

  • Information you provide directly: When you submit our Request a Quote or Contact forms, we collect details such as your name, company name, phone number, email address, product and quantity requirements, and any message you send us. If you contact us via WhatsApp, we receive whatever information you choose to share in that conversation.
  • Information collected automatically: Like most websites, our hosting provider automatically logs standard technical information when you visit — such as your IP address, browser type, device type, and the pages you view. This is used only for site operation, security, and troubleshooting.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Respond to quote requests and general enquiries
  • Communicate with you about your order or enquiry, including via phone, email, or WhatsApp
  • Improve our website and the products and services we offer
  • Meet legal, accounting, and regulatory requirements

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

Cookies and Similar Technologies

Our website may use small text files (cookies) or similar technologies for basic site functionality, such as remembering your menu preferences or preventing spam submissions on our forms. If we add analytics or advertising services in the future (see "Advertising" below), those services may also set cookies as described in this section, and we will update this policy accordingly. You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings; disabling them may affect how parts of the site function.

Third-Party Services We Use

Our website relies on a small number of trusted third-party services to operate:

  • Netlify — hosts our website and processes form submissions from our Contact and Request a Quote pages.
  • Google Maps — embedded on our Contact page to show our location. Google may collect data according to its own privacy policy when you interact with the map.
  • WhatsApp — used for direct messaging when you click our WhatsApp links; conversations are subject to WhatsApp's own privacy practices.
  • Instagram, Facebook, and X (Twitter) — linked from our site; visiting these platforms is subject to their respective privacy policies.

Each of these providers has its own privacy practices, and we encourage you to review them if you have concerns.

Advertising

We may in the future display advertisements on this website through services such as Google AdSense. Google and its partners may use cookies, including the DoubleClick cookie, to serve ads based on your visits to this and other websites, and to measure ad performance. You can learn more about how Google uses this data and manage your ad preferences at policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites, and opt out of personalised advertising through Google Ads Settings. We will update this section with further detail once any advertising service is active on the site.

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not share your personal information with third parties except: with service providers who help us operate our website and respond to enquiries (such as our hosting provider), when required by law or to protect our legal rights, or with your consent.

Data Retention

We retain the information you submit through our forms for as long as necessary to respond to your enquiry and maintain business records, typically in line with standard accounting and tax record-keeping requirements under Indian law, after which it is deleted or anonymised.

Your Rights

Depending on applicable law — including India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 — you may have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your personal information, or to withdraw consent for us to process it. To exercise any of these rights, contact us using the details below.

Children's Privacy

Our website and services are directed at businesses and individuals seeking commercial paper packaging products. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Data Security

We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect the information you share with us. However, no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal reasons. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect the most recent revision.
