How It Works

From requirement to delivery

Custom printing is available on every product we manufacture. Here's how a typical order moves forward.

1

Share your requirement

Tell us the product (paper bags, covers, cups or tissues), material preference (kraft, recycled or virgin), size and quantity you need — via the quote form or WhatsApp.

2

Share your design

Send your logo or artwork for printing. If you don't have a print-ready design yet, let us know and we'll guide you on what's needed.

3

Confirm pricing & quantity

We'll get back to you with pricing based on material, print complexity and order quantity, along with an estimated lead time.

4

Production

Once confirmed, we manufacture and print your order to the agreed specification.

5

Delivery

Your finished, custom printed packaging is delivered to you, ready to use.
